Mini Guide to Hong Kong

A brief introduction to the bustling city of Hong Kong, including food, malls and theme parks!

FOOD

Hong Kong is known for its street food. You can enjoy cheap but delicious snacks as you walk along the streets of Hong Kong! Here is a brief introduction to the classic street foods:

Egglets (雞蛋仔)

Egglets are a local Hong Kong food, resembling inverted, rounded waffles. They can be found in street stalls all over Hong Kong.
Besides plain flavor, many variations such as green tea, chocolate and chocolate chip egglets can be found.
Lately, egglets have been featured as a component of extravagent sundaes and parfaits. You can find these at restaurants like Oddies Foodies!
